Basil Seeds

Basil seeds are tiny black seeds that grow into aromatic basil plants, commonly used in cooking and herbal remedies.

Neem Seeds

Neem seeds grow into neem trees known for medicinal properties, pest control, and environmental benefits.

Mango Seeds

Mango seeds are large seeds that can grow into mango trees, giving sweet tropical fruits enjoyed worldwide.
