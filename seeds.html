Seed Species

Wheat

A major cereal crop, used for making flour.

Rice

Staple food crop, grown in paddies.

Maize

Used for food, fodder, and industrial products.

Soybean

Protein-rich seed used in food and oil.

Chickpea

Legume, rich in protein, used in curries.

Lentil

Small legume, widely consumed in soups and dals.

Pea

Green vegetable seed, used in soups and curries.

Mustard

Used in cooking oils and as a spice.

Sunflower

Used for oil and snacks.

Barley

Cereal grain used for food and brewing.
